## How to Use This Folder
- Each folder has a `lesson.py` (concepts with examples) and `exercises.py` (practice problems)
- Open `lesson.py`, read the code, then **run it** with `F5` or the ▶ button in VS Code
- Try `exercises.py` on your own — solutions are at the bottom (hidden in comments)

## Tips
- Use `print()` everywhere to see what's happening
- **Read the error messages** — Python's errors are very descriptive
- Don't memorize syntax — just understand the concept and look up when needed
